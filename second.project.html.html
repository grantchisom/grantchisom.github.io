Microdose Capsule

Microdosing involves taking a very small dose of some popular hallucinogenic substances. Proponents of Microdosing believe that the practice benefits the mind, and research has recently started to explore this possibility

However, the more common term for Microdosing-a simple fraction of a "normal dose"-is rather vague, as it does not provide a defined numerical value. Therefore, the common accepted definition is a small dose that does not cause any intoxicating or significant alteration of consciousness

The intention of Microdosing also differs from that of normal psychedelic or hallucinogenic substances taking. Many people use psychedelics at higher doses for self-exploration, recreation, or spiritual purposes. On the other hand, microdosers tend to take these doses to improve their well-being or enhance their emotional or mental state.
